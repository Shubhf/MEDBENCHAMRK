Fellow medical AI researcher here. I just shipped MedResearch Mind — a tool that finds research gaps across medical AI papers.

I built it because I spent 4 weeks doing a lit review on retinal imaging, missed obvious gaps, and then found 3 papers published in the gap I didn't explore. Never again.

What it does:
- Drop in your papers (PubMed, ArXiv, MICCAI talks, etc.)
- Get gaps ranked by clinical relevance and feasibility
- Each gap comes with a PICO experiment proposal
- Zero hallucination — every claim cites specific papers

It's free: https://medresearchmind.app
