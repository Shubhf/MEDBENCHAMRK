## Description (250 words)

MedResearch Mind is a research intelligence platform built specifically for medical AI researchers.

Unlike generic research tools, MedResearch Mind understands medical imaging modalities, clinical datasets, evaluation metrics, and can design PICO-formatted experiments from identified gaps.

Key Features:
- Medical AI Gap Finder: Upload 5-50 papers, get ranked research gaps with clinical relevance scores and feasibility estimates
- Universal Input: PubMed, ArXiv, bioRxiv, YouTube (conference talks), GitHub, ClinicalTrials.gov
- Zero Hallucination Q&A: Every answer cites paper + section + page. Confidence scores on every response
- PICO Experiment Designer: From gap to experiment proposal in PICO format
- Medical Knowledge Memory: Remembers your clinical focus, tracks modalities and conditions across sessions
- MedResearchBench: Open benchmark for evaluating AI on medical research tasks

Built by Shubh Garg — 11 peer-reviewed publications in medical AI including BMC Ophthalmology, IEEE, and Elsevier. 2 patents. Research at Samsung PRISM, IIT Jodhpur, and IIM Udaipur.

Free tier for PhD students and academic labs. Pro at $9/month.
